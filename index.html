Welcome to BR Poultry Farms

About Us

Established on November 17th, 2017, BR Poultry Farms has been a trusted name in the poultry industry for over eight years. We specialize in layer birds for hatching eggs and are known for maintaining top-tier equipment and a hygienic, eco-friendly environment that promotes optimal bird growth.

Our farm is proudly partnered with Sri Venkateswara Hatcheries since inception, ensuring the highest quality standards in poultry breeding and egg production.

BR Poultry Farms is led by our esteemed proprietor, Mr. Shaik Syed Basha, a highly talented engineer with over 25 years of experience serving in multinational petroleum companies. He is admired for his humility, hard work, and dedication, having built this venture entirely through his own efforts. He is widely respected for his simplicity and inspiring journey.

Supporting him is his wife, Dr. S. Razia, a respected government school headmistress and an active participant in social welfare initiatives. She has received numerous accolades for her outstanding contributions to society.

The couple has two sons: Mr. Babji, the Managing Director of BR Poultry Farms, holds a B.Tech from SRM University and an MBA. Their younger son, Dr. S. S. Rehaman, is an MBBS graduate from the prestigious GITAM University and is currently pursuing his MD in Pharmacology at Narayana Medical College.

Founder Profile

Mr. Shaik Syed Basha, the visionary founder and proprietor of BR Poultry Farms, is a seasoned engineer with over 25 years of experience in global petroleum companies. His exceptional journey is marked by hard work, humility, and a passion for excellence. Known for his simplicity and strong values, he laid the foundation for BR Poultry Farms with unwavering dedication and purpose.
